DOCUMENT TRACKING SYSTEM (DTS)

A Document Tracking System is a digital solution for managing documents efficiently and securely, ensuring that every step in a document’s lifecycle is logged and traceable. It is vital for modern organizations that handle large volumes of files and need compliance-ready processes. DOCUMENT DEPOSITORY SYSTEM (DDS)

A Document Depository System is a structured platform or repository designed to store, organize, manage, and provide controlled access to documents and records. It acts as a centralized hub where physical or digital documents are deposited, cataloged, and retrieved when needed.

DDS

POSTAL PROFILE SYSTEM (PPS)

A Postal Profile System is a structured framework or database used by a postal organization to collect, store, and manage information about customers, employees, and postal transactions. It acts as a centralized system that maintains detailed profiles to improve service delivery, operational efficiency, and accountability.
